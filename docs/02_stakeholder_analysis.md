# Stakeholder Analysis

The solution was designed with multiple stakeholders in mind, each with distinct goals and constraints.

## Students
- Discover active sustainability research topics
- Identify under-researched SDGs
- Find potential faculty collaborators

## Faculty
- Identify collaborators with complementary methods and interests
- Track departmental sustainability research trends
- Discover interdisciplinary opportunities

## PhD Candidates & Researchers
- Search for potential advisors or research assistantships
- Evaluate professor availability and research focus

## College Leadership & Donors
- Assess institutional sustainability strengths
- Understand research impact and gaps
- Support strategic funding decisions

Designing for these stakeholders ensured the solution balanced usability, insight depth, and strategic value.
