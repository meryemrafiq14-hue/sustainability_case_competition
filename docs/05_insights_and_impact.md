# Insights and Impact

Key insights from the analysis include:
- Certain SDGs receive significantly more research attention than others
- Collaboration opportunities exist across departments that rarely interact
- Early-career researchers benefit from structured discovery tools

## Impact
- Improves visibility of sustainability expertise
- Reduces friction in forming interdisciplinary teams
- Supports data-driven decision making for leadership and donors

The solution moves the institution from passive information access to proactive insight generation.
